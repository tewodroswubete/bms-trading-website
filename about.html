About BMS Trading PLC

Manufacturing Excellence Since 2000 E.C. - Leading the Way in Plastic and Soap Production

Our Story

Founded in 2000 E.C., BMS Trading PLC emerged from a vision to transform Ethiopia's manufacturing landscape. What started as a small operation in Alemgena has grown into one of the country's most trusted manufacturers of plastic and soap products.

Over the past 25 years, we have consistently delivered quality products that meet the diverse needs of Ethiopian households and businesses. Our commitment to excellence has earned us the trust of over 1,000 customers across six major cities.

Today, with our state-of-the-art facility in Sheger City and headquarters in Addis Ababa, we continue to innovate and expand our product range, staying true to our founding principles of quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction.
